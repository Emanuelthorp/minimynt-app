## What MiniMynt Is
A structured family economy platform that turns household chores into real earnings.
Parents own and control the system. Children complete tasks. Payment is real money via Vipps (0.5% fee).

## What MiniMynt Is NOT
- Not a bank
- Not a gamified app (no points, no fake currency, no streaks)
- Not a kids' wallet or savings app
- Not a social platform

## Core Principles
1. Parent-created accounts only
2. Permanent role separation (Child or Adult)
3. Phone-based login (Vipps for adults)
4. Clear task-to-payment logic: Available → Taken → Awaiting approval → Approved → Paid
5. Effort is measurable; work connects directly to real value

## Target
- Nordic families (Norway first) with children aged 6–16
- Parents who want to teach economic thinking through real participation

## Tone
- Clear, direct, Scandinavian. No hype, no cute, no corporate.
- "This is how money works" — pedagogical but not patronizing.

## Constraints
- v1 must be buildable in 14 days by a solo founder
- Vipps is the only payment rail
- Norwegian market first (NOK only)
- Mobile-first responsive web app (no native app for v1)

## What Good Looks Like
- A parent can set up their family, create tasks, approve work, and pay in under 5 minutes
- A child can see available tasks, claim one, mark it done, and see real money arrive
- Zero ambiguity in the task lifecycle
- Real money moves, not tokens

## Boundaries
- No AI features in v1 (no task suggestions, no smart pricing)
- No social features (no leaderboards, no sharing)
- No marketplace (parents define all tasks)
- No recurring subscriptions for users in v1
